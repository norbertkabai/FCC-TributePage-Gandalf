Gandalf

  • Who is Gandalf?

    Gandalf the Grey is an Istar (Wizard), sent to Middle-earth by the Valar, to combat Sauron. He was sent in the Third Age, along with Saruman the White, Radagast the Brown, and 2 blue wizards, whose names have been lost. He joined Thorin Oakshields company, to help him reclaim his homeland, the Lonely Mountains from the dragon named Smaug. After achieving that, he spent his time wandering, and obtaining information about the One Ring. Eventually he arrived to the Shire, to visit his old friend from the above mentioned company, Bilbo Baggins. Gandalf also helped to form and was part of the Fellowship of the Ring, saved the Fellowship from a Balrog at the cost of his own life, then he was sent back by Eru and became Gandalf the White. He fought alongside the people of Rohan at Helm's Deep, took part in the battle of Gondor, and led the Free Peoples against Sauron in the War of the Ring, where they emerged victorious.
